About Mandy Mason

Mandy Mason is currently a software development student at Code Fellows, and is enrolled in the nights and weekends section of code 201. During the day, Mandy is an Elementary School Music Teacher in Stuart, Florida. She lives in Port Saint Lucie, Florida with her husband Daniel, and their cat Gizmo.

Education

Mandy was born in Nashville, Tennessee where she attended several K-8 schools around the Middle Tennessee area, and completed high school in Lebanon, Tennessee (the home of Cracker Barrel!). After graduating high school in 2004, Mandy attended college at Austin Peay State University in Clarksville, Tennessee to study Physics and Mathematics.

She quickly realized that she got much more enjoyment out of her music classes and ensembles. After a two-year break from school, she changed her major to Flute Performance and Music Education, and re-enrolled at APSU. Mandy graduated cum laude in December of 2013.
